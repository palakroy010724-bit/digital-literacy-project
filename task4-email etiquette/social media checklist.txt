Do’s:
•	Check your privacy settings periodically so you know precisely who is seeing your posts.
•	Shout out to some achievements like having Certifications for Python, having good GitHub profiles, etc.
•	Connect with the industry professionals and alumni from your domain like AIML and data science etc. And get the latest trends in the field.
•	Read and check a source before posting it to avoid being a digital information misinformer.
•	Upload a professional picture for your profile on sites like LinkedIn so you create a good first impression.
•	Tag other students in the sensitive pictures that you upload without their prior consent.
Don’ts’: 
•	Do not post sensitive and confidential information such as your Student ID number, home address, private project keys, etc.

•	Get involved with arguments and flame wars that negatively portray your emotional intelligence.
•	Do not post pictures and videos that involve criminal activities or violate the academic integrity code of your institution.
•	Talk ill about your professors or boss as this will be perceived by recruiters as not being able to handle professional situations.
